EventSpase

A single home for all your events

Informing and updating people about an event across different channels is difficult and time-consuming. Sharing events is an upstream battle for attention. We were tired of constant reposting, food pics, and CandyCrush invites; so we built EventSpase.

With EventSpase, we provide a forum for people to create and share event information, quickly and easily. It's a space where events can exist without all that other noise. Best of all,

It just works. Coming Fall 2020.
