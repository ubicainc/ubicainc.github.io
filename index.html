More than just events. Experiences

Millennials have a desire to facilitate and host events that are engaging and meaningful - without the work of being a promoter; and an unprecedented enthusiasm to engage in these events - without the work of actively looking for them.

Designed For The Modern Student


There are only three things today’s youth care about: themselves, the lives of other people and the desire to find meaning in this socially revolutionary period in history. The advent of social media provided us with a stimulating new way to engage with life, providing us with the power to create new identities, exercise our influence on the world and getting to ‘know more’ about others.

This was engaging for a decade, however, our internal desires are shifting. We are awakening and going offline in search for something more. We have a growing desire for genuine live experiences and human connection.

This longing - coupled with the increased desire of community organizations, businesses and institutions to develop more intimate relationships with youth - provides Ubica with a unique opportunity to foster connection through the event experience.
